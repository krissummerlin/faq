How do I use "clean"?

Booking a house cleaning with the Clean app is simple as it gets! Just open the app, choose the amount of hours you want, Next choose a time and a date you want your cleaning, enter your info and thats it!

Do I need to enter my information every time I book?

Nope! When you book the first time your account is created and the information is stored, so the next time you can book at the touch of a button. If however something needs to be changed (like special instructions) you can change it anytime

Will my cleaner be a professional?

Yes! All of our cleaners are background checked and tested in person to ensure qulity cleanings and great customers experience abilities.

What if I'm not happy with my cleaner?

Don't worry, we've already thought of that. Though we would love to keep everyone perfectly happy, things happen. So if you're not happy with your cleaner we will make sure you don't get that cleaner again. We will alos give you a free re-clean when ever you want it!
